About This Project

Monitor and Dead Pixel Test is an independent browser-based diagnostic toolkit for display buyers, gamers, and professionals. Our goal is to provide reproducible visual checks without requiring desktop installation.

Editorial Principles

We prioritize practical validation over marketing claims. Each test mode documents what it can and cannot detect. We avoid inflated benchmark claims and focus on repeatable visual workflows across LCD, OLED, and mini-LED panels.

Update Policy

Patterns, guidance text, and diagnostic logic are updated when we verify meaningful improvements. Major updates are reflected in homepage timestamp and changelog notes.
